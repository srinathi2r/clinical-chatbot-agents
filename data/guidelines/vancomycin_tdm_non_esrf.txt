VANCOMYCIN DOSING - NON-ESRF PATIENTS (not on haemodialysis, SLED, or peritoneal dialysis)

LOADING DOSE
Weight-based loading dose: 25-30 mg/kg (actual body weight)
Round each individual dose to the nearest 250 mg.
Single dose ceiling: 3000 mg (3 g). If calculated loading dose exceeds 3 g, cap at 3 g and state: "Single dose capped at 3 g per guideline ceiling."

MAINTENANCE DOSING
Initial frequency: Q12H for most patients with normal or mildly impaired renal function.
Round each individual dose to the nearest 250 mg.
Single dose ceiling: 3000 mg per individual dose.

CRITICAL RULE - DAILY DOSE CEILING:
1500 mg Q12H = exactly 3 g/day. This does NOT exceed the daily ceiling.
If calculated total daily dose using Q12H dosing would exceed 3 g/day, switch to Q8H strategy rather than increasing Q12H per-dose amount.
1500 mg Q12H is safe and does not trigger frequency change.
Do NOT describe 3 g/day as exceeding 3 g/day.

ROUNDING RULE (deterministic, mandatory):
Step 1: Calculate weight-based dose.
Step 2: Round to nearest 250 mg. Examples: 1225 mg rounds to 1250 mg. 1100 mg rounds to 1000 mg. 1375 mg rounds to 1500 mg.
Step 3: Check ceiling: if rounded dose > 3000 mg, cap at 3000 mg.
Step 4: State rounding and ceiling check.

TROUGH MONITORING
Target trough: typically 10-20 mg/L for most infections. Consult site-specific guideline for target.
Timing of trough: draw immediately before the next dose (within 30 minutes).
Do NOT extrapolate dialysis TDM algorithms. Use only this document for non-ESRF patients.

RE-DOSING / DOSE ADJUSTMENT
When re-dosing table gives a percentage range: calculate and round both ends of the range. Present the rounded acceptable range. Do not choose upper or lower end unless guideline gives selection criteria.
